Our Commitment to Privacy

ShopEase respects your privacy and is fully committed to protecting your personal data in compliance with international privacy laws.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. We ensure that only necessary data is collected to optimize your experience. This includes:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email address, phone number, and billing/shipping details.
  • Account Data: Encrypted passwords, preferences, and profile images.
  • Transactional Data: Order history, wishlists, and cart contents.
  • Usage Data: IP addresses, browser types, and interactions on our platform to help us improve user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used primarily to process your orders, improve our services, and personalize your shopping journey. Specific uses include:

  • Fulfilling your orders and sending delivery updates.
  • Providing customer support and responding to inquiries.
  • Detecting and preventing fraudulent activities to ensure platform safety.
  • Sending promotional offers and newsletters (only if you have opted in, which you can easily opt-out of at any time).

3. Data Security

We implement a variety of rigorous security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. All payment transactions are encrypted using secure socket layer (SSL) technology and processed through verified gateways like Razorpay. Your data is stored in secure, certified cloud networks accessible only by a limited number of authorized personnel who undergo strict background checks and are required to keep the information confidential.

4. Sharing Your Information

We strictly do not sell, trade, or rent your personally identifiable information to outside parties for marketing purposes. We only share data with trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business (like logistics and delivery partners), or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to binding data processing agreements to keep this information strictly confidential and secure.

5. Your Rights & Consent

By using our site, you consent to our privacy policy. You have the right to access, modify, or permanently delete your personal data at any time. If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page and notify you via email if the changes are significant. For any requests regarding your data rights, please contact our support team.
