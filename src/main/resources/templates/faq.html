Orders & Shipping

Check your email for a tracking link or visit your profile dashboard for real-time updates. You can also chat with our AI assistant for quick tracking.

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide using premium courier services like FedEx and DHL. Additional customs fees may apply depending on your region.

Address changes are only possible within 1 hour of placing an order before processing begins. Please contact support immediately if you need to update your details.

Shipping is absolutely FREE for orders above Rs. 999. For smaller orders, a flat nominal fee of Rs. 100 applies to cover logistics costs.

Standard delivery typically takes 3-5 business days depending on your location and regional logistics. Express 1-day delivery is available in select metro cities.

Returns & Refunds

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy for unused items in original packaging with all tags intact. Just raise a request from your order history.

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after our quality check team inspects the returned item. The amount will be credited back to your original payment method.

Returns are completely FREE if the item received was damaged, defective, or incorrect. For size exchanges or change of mind, a minimal pickup fee may apply.

Innerwear, hygiene products, personalized items, and clearance sale items are generally non-returnable due to safety protocols and custom nature.
